The idea

Not another chatbot. A personal system that lives where life already happens.

etgar.ai is a working pilot of a private personal OS with long-term memory. It creates and manages a readable second-brain vault that stays active across daily life. See what exists today.

How it works

Three ideas make it different.

01

It remembers from sources

The assistant builds and maintains a second-brain vault from real notes, logs, tasks, and records, not vague chat memory. Important facts are traceable back to where they came from.

02

It coordinates across life

It can work across email, calendar, Trello, WhatsApp, Telegram, grocery lists, travel plans, vendor tracking, and Home Assistant scenes, lights, sensors, cameras, cleaning robots, watering, alert systems, and smart routines that coordinate across the home.

03

It stays inspectable

The system is built around clear permissions. Anything that enters long-term memory requires human approval, and control of the knowledge always stays with you. It is model-agnostic rather than locked to one provider.
